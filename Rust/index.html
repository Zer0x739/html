In Rust, players start with nothing but a rock and a torch, and must quickly gather materials such as wood, stone, and metal to construct their first base. The game features a complex crafting system that allows players to create tools, weapons, and structures to aid in their survival. Gathering resources and managing hunger, thirst, and health are crucial aspects of gameplay.


One of the defining features of Rust is its PvP aspect. Players must be constantly vigilant, as other players can pose a significant threat. Raiding and looting other players' bases is a common strategy, leading to intense battles and territorial disputes. This dynamic creates a tense and unpredictable environment where cooperation and betrayal are equally viable strategies.
